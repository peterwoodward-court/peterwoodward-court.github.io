I am a medical doctor currently reading for a PhD in AI enabled Healthcare Systems at University College London. I am interested in image classification models, as well as addressing the barriers to deploying AI in the clinical environment.

I am also interested in medical education both to current students and the wider public. I have had several roles related to these interests, including panel membership of the Excellence in Medical Education Awards, interviewing for UCL medical school undergraduates, chair of my university's Education Society, and was Editor-in-Chief for The Medical Student newspaper. In 2018, I was nominated for Teacher of the Year by the University of Oxford Clinical School. I am currently developing a YouTube channel that aims to teach adults about the workings of the human body.

I am an amateur photographer and pianist.
